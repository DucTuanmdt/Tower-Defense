You must protect your territory by using towers to kill enemy's tank.
Using mouse, left double click to choose function you want (buy or sell your tower)
and left click on invalid land (GRAY color) to put, and click on tower to sell it
with half price.
Press Space button to Pause and Enter to Continue.
Press ESC to back Menu.
If out of time and you still life you will Win!
Good luck!!!
